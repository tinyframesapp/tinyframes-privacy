TinyFrames Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2025

TinyFrames respects and protects the privacy of all users, including children under 13. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected and used.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. All photos, notes, and videos remain on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

2. Camera Access

Camera access is only used to allow you to capture baby photos. We do not store, process, or share camera data.

3. Children’s Privacy

TinyFrames is designed for families and children under 13. No data is collected, stored, or transmitted from children. We fully comply with COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act).

4. In-App Purchases

If you choose to upgrade, payment is handled securely by the App Store / Google Play. No credit card or billing info is handled by us directly.

5. No Third-Party Services

This app does not use advertising or third-party analytics.

6. Data Retention

All data stays on your device unless you choose to export it.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please email us at: tinyframesapp@gmail.com
